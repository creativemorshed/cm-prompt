==============================
CINEMATIC TRANSITION LIBRARY
==============================

[1] FADE IN
Scene gradually appears from darkness.

Use For:
- Opening scenes
- Emotional moments

--------------------------------------------------

[2] FADE OUT
Scene slowly disappears into black.

Use For:
- Endings
- Reflection

--------------------------------------------------

[3] SMOOTH DISSOLVE
One scene blends into another.

Use For:
- Time passage
- Memories

--------------------------------------------------

[4] MATCH CUT
Similar shapes connect two scenes.

Use For:
- Symbolism
- Story flow

--------------------------------------------------

[5] WHIP PAN TRANSITION
Fast camera movement between scenes.

Use For:
- Action
- Tension
- Pattern interrupts

--------------------------------------------------

[6] GLITCH TRANSITION
Digital distortion effect.

Use For:
- Technology
- System failure
- Surveillance

--------------------------------------------------

[7] ZOOM TRANSITION
Camera zoom connects scenes.

Use For:
- Reveals
- Emotional escalation

--------------------------------------------------

[8] LIGHT SWEEP TRANSITION
Bright light moves across the screen.

Use For:
- Hope
- Transformation

--------------------------------------------------

[9] MAP MORPH TRANSITION
One infographic transforms into another.

Use For:
- Statistics
- Educational scenes

--------------------------------------------------

[10] BLACK FRAME IMPACT CUT
Short black frame with sound impact.

Use For:
- Shock moments
- Important reveals

--------------------------------------------------

[11] PARALLAX TRANSITION
Layered movement creates depth.

Use For:
- Documentary style
- Slow pacing

--------------------------------------------------

[12] DOORWAY TRANSITION
Camera enters darkness or light through a doorway.

Use For:
- Mystery
- Ending hooks
