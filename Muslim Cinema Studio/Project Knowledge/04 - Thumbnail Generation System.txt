THUMBNAIL IMAGE RULES
- Single main subject
- Strong emotion
- Warm golden lighting
- Cinematic, high contrast
- Clean, mobile-friendly composition
- Large focal subject
- Dramatic atmosphere

AVOID
- Too many characters
- Small details
- Cluttered composition
- Text inside the AI-generated image (thumbnail text is added separately during editing)
- Prophet face depiction
- Offensive imagery
