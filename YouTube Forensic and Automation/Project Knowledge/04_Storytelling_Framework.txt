DOCUMENT TITLE:
Storytelling Framework (Professional Edition)

VERSION:
1.0

PURPOSE:

এই Framework-এর উদ্দেশ্য হলো High-Retention Story Structure তৈরি করা, যা Viewer-এর Attention ধরে রাখবে, Emotion তৈরি করবে এবং Watch Time বৃদ্ধি করবে।

Storytelling হলো Retention-এর সবচেয়ে শক্তিশালী Engine।

--------------------------------------------------

CORE PRINCIPLE

Facts Inform.

Stories Engage.

Emotions Retain.

মানুষ তথ্য মনে রাখে না।

মানুষ গল্প মনে রাখে।

- Curiosity

--------------------------------------------------

STAGE 1:
HOOK

Purpose:

Viewer Stop & Watch

--------------------------------------------------

GOAL

Attention Capture

Within:

0-15 Seconds

--------------------------------------------------

HOOK TYPES

1. Shock Hook

Example:

"মাত্র ৩০ সেকেন্ডে তার পুরো জীবন বদলে যায়।"

--------------------------------------------------

2. Mystery Hook

Example:

"এই ছবির পেছনের সত্য জানলে আপনি অবাক হবেন।"

--------------------------------------------------

3. Conflict Hook

Example:

"সবাই বলেছিল এটা অসম্ভব।"

--------------------------------------------------

4. Outcome Hook

Example:

"শেষে যা ঘটেছিল, কেউ কল্পনাও করেনি।"

--------------------------------------------------

RULE

Never explain.

Create curiosity.

--------------------------------------------------

STAGE 2:
SETUP

Purpose:

Introduce the situation.

--------------------------------------------------

SETUP ELEMENTS

- Character
- Situation
- Goal
- Context

--------------------------------------------------

VIEWER SHOULD UNDERSTAND

Who?

What?

Why?

--------------------------------------------------

RULE

Keep setup short.

Move quickly to conflict.

--------------------------------------------------

STAGE 3:
CONFLICT

Purpose:

Create tension.

--------------------------------------------------

NO CONFLICT

=

NO STORY

--------------------------------------------------

CONFLICT TYPES

Person vs Person

Person vs Society

Person vs Nature

Person vs Technology

Person vs Self

--------------------------------------------------

WHY CONFLICT MATTERS

Conflict creates:

- Uncertainty
- Curiosity
- Emotional investment

--------------------------------------------------

STAGE 4:
ESCALATION

Purpose:

Increase tension.

--------------------------------------------------

BAD STORY

Problem stays same.

--------------------------------------------------

GOOD STORY

Problem gets bigger.

--------------------------------------------------

ESCALATION MODEL

Small Problem

↓

Bigger Problem

↓

Serious Problem

↓

Critical Problem

--------------------------------------------------

RULE

Every few minutes:

Raise the stakes.

--------------------------------------------------

STAGE 5:
TWIST

Purpose:

Break viewer expectations.

--------------------------------------------------

TWIST DEFINITION

Expected Outcome

↓

Unexpected Reality

--------------------------------------------------

TWIST TYPES

Hidden Truth

Betrayal

Unexpected Discovery

Role Reversal

False Assumption

--------------------------------------------------

WHY TWISTS WORK

Brain loves surprises.

Surprise creates:

- Attention
- Emotion
- Retention

--------------------------------------------------

STAGE 6:
CLIMAX

Purpose:

Peak emotional moment.

--------------------------------------------------

CLIMAX QUESTIONS

Will they succeed?

Will they fail?

What happens now?

--------------------------------------------------

CLIMAX CHARACTERISTICS

Highest tension

Highest emotion

Highest stakes

--------------------------------------------------

RULE

Never rush the climax.

--------------------------------------------------

STAGE 7:
RESOLUTION

Purpose:

Deliver payoff.

--------------------------------------------------

VIEWER WANTS

Closure.

--------------------------------------------------

RESOLUTION SHOULD ANSWER

What happened?

Why it happened?

What changed?

--------------------------------------------------

RULE

Pay off major questions.

--------------------------------------------------

STAGE 8:
ENDING HOOK

Purpose:

Extend session watch time.

--------------------------------------------------

EXAMPLES

"But this wasn't the end..."

"What happened next was even crazier..."

"This led to another mystery..."

--------------------------------------------------

WHY IT WORKS

Creates:

New curiosity loop

↓

More viewing

↓

More session watch time

STORY MOMENTUM SYSTEM

Every scene must do at least one:

- Introduce information
- Increase tension
- Create curiosity
- Advance conflict
- Deliver payoff

--------------------------------------------------

SCENE QUALITY TEST

Ask:

If this scene is removed,

does the story become weaker?

If NO,

remove it.

--------------------------------------------------

COMMON STORYTELLING MISTAKES

No Stakes

Predictability

No Escalation

No Twist

Weak Ending

No Payoff

--------------------------------------------------

FORENSIC ANALYSIS RULES

Never say:

"The story is good."

Instead explain:

- Why it is good
- Which emotional triggers are used
- Which retention systems are used
- Which story mechanics are used
- Whether the structure is repeatable
