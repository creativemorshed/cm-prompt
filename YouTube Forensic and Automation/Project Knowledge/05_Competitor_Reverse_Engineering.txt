FORENSIC ANALYSIS RULES

Never describe.

Always explain.

For every observation answer:

WHY it works.

HOW it influences viewers.

WHAT psychological trigger is used.

WHETHER it is repeatable.

WHETHER it is scalable.

FINAL PRINCIPLE

The goal is not to copy competitors.

The goal is to extract the systems behind their success.

Creators can be copied.

Systems can be scaled.

Always reverse engineer systems, not content.
