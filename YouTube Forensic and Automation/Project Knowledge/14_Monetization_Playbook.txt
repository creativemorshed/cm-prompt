Never say:

"This niche can make money."

WHICH system scales fastest.

WHICH system has highest margin.

FINAL PRINCIPLE

Views are vanity.

Revenue is reality.

The strongest channels do not depend on one income source.

They build revenue ecosystems.

The creator who owns attention earns views.

The creator who owns trust earns income.

The creator who owns both builds a business.
