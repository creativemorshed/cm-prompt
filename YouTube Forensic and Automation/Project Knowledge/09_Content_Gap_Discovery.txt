THE 5 TYPES OF CONTENT GAPS

1. Information Gap

2. Perspective Gap

3. Format Gap

4. Audience Gap

5. Trend Gap

--------------------------------------------------

GAP TYPE #1

INFORMATION GAP

Definition:

Audience wants answers.

Answers do not exist.

--------------------------------------------------

Examples

Unsolved Mystery

New Discovery

Unknown History

Hidden Information

--------------------------------------------------

QUESTIONS

What questions remain unanswered?

What information is missing?

--------------------------------------------------

GAP TYPE #2

PERSPECTIVE GAP

Definition:

Everyone covers the topic.

Nobody covers it this way.

--------------------------------------------------

Examples

Different angle

Contrarian viewpoint

Alternative explanation

--------------------------------------------------

QUESTIONS

What perspective is missing?

What assumptions can be challenged?

--------------------------------------------------

GAP TYPE #3

FORMAT GAP

Definition:

Topic exists.

Format is weak.

--------------------------------------------------

Examples

Text → Documentary

Facts → Story

Research → Investigation

--------------------------------------------------

QUESTIONS

Can this topic be presented better?

--------------------------------------------------

GAP TYPE #4

AUDIENCE GAP

Definition:

Certain viewers are ignored.

--------------------------------------------------

Examples

Beginners

Advanced users

Specific age groups

Specific countries

--------------------------------------------------

QUESTIONS

Who is not being served?

--------------------------------------------------

GAP TYPE #5

TREND GAP

Definition:

Demand is rising.

Supply is limited.

--------------------------------------------------

Examples

Emerging technology

Breaking story

New movement

Cultural shift

--------------------------------------------------

QUESTIONS

What is growing right now?
