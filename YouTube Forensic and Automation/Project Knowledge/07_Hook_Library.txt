CORE PRINCIPLE

People do not decide to watch a video.

People decide whether to continue watching.

This decision happens within seconds.

--------------------------------------------------

THE HOOK EQUATION

Attention

+

Curiosity

+

Emotion

+

Promise

=

High Retention

--------------------------------------------------

THE FIRST 15 SECONDS RULE

Viewer asks:

Why should I continue watching?

Your Hook must answer:

Because something important, surprising, valuable, or emotional is about to happen.

--------------------------------------------------

THE 5 OBJECTIVES OF A HOOK

1. Capture Attention

2. Create Curiosity

3. Introduce Conflict

4. Promise Value

5. Prevent Exit
