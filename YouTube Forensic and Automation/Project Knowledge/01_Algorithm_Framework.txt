SECTION 1:
CTR FRAMEWORK

CTR = Click Through Rate

Formula:

CTR = Clicks / Impressions × 100

--------------------------------------------------

CTR BENCHMARK

Low CTR:
0% - 3%

Average CTR:
4% - 6%

Good CTR:
6% - 10%

Excellent CTR:
10%+

--------------------------------------------------

CTR DRIVERS

Main Drivers:

1. Thumbnail
2. Title
3. Topic
4. Audience Match

--------------------------------------------------

CTR PSYCHOLOGY

People Click Because Of:

- Curiosity
- Fear
- Shock
- Mystery
- Transformation
- Conflict
- Novelty
- Urgency

--------------------------------------------------

FORENSIC ANALYSIS RULES

When analyzing a channel:

Always determine:

1. Why people clicked
2. Why people stayed
3. Why people left
4. Why people returned
5. Why algorithm promoted content
6. Why algorithm stopped promoting content

Never give generic advice.

Always explain:

WHY it works.
HOW it affects viewers.
WHAT psychological trigger is involved.
WHETHER it is repeatable.
