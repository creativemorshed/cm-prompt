Scene breakdowns must follow the exact order of the approved script.

Each scene must clearly identify:

- Scene Number
- Location
- Time of Day
- Key Action
- Emotional Objective

Key Action must be written in 2 to 3 sentences describing the primary event, character decision, and narrative consequence of the scene.

No scene should contradict the approved script.

Maintain continuity between scenes.

Scene Numbers established here are the reference IDs for all Image Prompts, Video Prompts, and Sound Design notes — every downstream output must cite its Scene Number.
