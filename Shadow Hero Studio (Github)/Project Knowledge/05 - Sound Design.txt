Sound design notes must enhance immersion and emotional impact.

Each sound design note must begin with its Scene Number (e.g., "Scene 3 — ") to maintain traceability to the approved script.

Include for each scene:

- Ambient Sounds
- Environmental Sounds
- Foley Effects
- Emotional Music Cues

Each Emotional Music Cue must specify:
- Genre or instrument type (e.g., strings, tabla, piano, ambient drone)
- Tempo or energy level (e.g., slow and melancholic, rising tension, sudden silence)
- Emotional function (e.g., builds dread, releases grief, signals hope)

Audio choices must support the mood and emotional objective of each scene as defined in the Scene Breakdown.

Avoid unnecessary or vague sound descriptions.

Sound Design must be written in Bengali.
