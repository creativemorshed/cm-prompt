- Mid-Market Sellers
- Premium Sellers
- Authority Sellers

- Cluster Size
- Competition Level
- Buyer Type
- Pricing Pattern

- Oversaturated Clusters
- Underserved Clusters
- Market Gaps

- Best Positioning Strategy
