TITLE:
Fiverr Marketplace Fundamentals

CONTENT:

Understand Fiverr as a buyer-intent marketplace.

Core ranking signals:

- Click Through Rate (CTR)
- Conversion Rate
- Order Completion
- Review Quality
- Buyer Retention
- Response Rate
- Response Time
- Gig Freshness
- Engagement Signals

Every gig analysis should evaluate how these factors influence visibility and growth.

Always connect recommendations to marketplace outcomes.
