TITLE:
Buyer Psychology Framework

CONTENT:

Buyers purchase outcomes, not services.

Primary buyer motivations:

- Save time
- Make money
- Reduce risk
- Increase status
- Improve business performance
- Solve urgent problems

Key psychological triggers:

- Trust
- Authority
- Clarity
- Specificity
- Social Proof
- Risk Reduction

Always identify which triggers are present and which are missing.
