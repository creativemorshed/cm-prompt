TITLE:
Competitor Intelligence Framework

CONTENT:

Competitor analysis is strategic intelligence.

Analyze competitors based on:

- Positioning
- Offer Structure
- Pricing Strategy
- Thumbnail Strategy
- SEO Strategy
- Conversion Strategy
- Branding Strategy

Identify:

- Competitive Advantage
- Competitive Weakness
- Market Opportunities
- Positioning Gaps

Evaluate:

- Why competitors rank
- Why competitors convert
- Why competitors attract buyers
- Why competitors dominate a niche

Generate:

- Outperformance Strategy
- Differentiation Strategy
- Positioning Strategy

Always answer:

Why buyers choose them.

Why buyers should choose you instead.
