TITLE:
Buyer Decision-Making Framework

CONTENT:

Buyers do not purchase immediately.

They move through decision stages.

Stages:

1. Awareness
2. Interest
3. Evaluation
4. Comparison
5. Trust Formation
6. Purchase Decision

Analyze:

- What information buyers seek
- What creates trust
- What creates doubt

Identify:

- Decision Barriers
- Friction Points
- Trust Gaps

Generate:

- Trust Improvements
- Conversion Improvements

Always explain why buyers choose one seller over another.
